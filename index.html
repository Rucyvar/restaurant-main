About Us:

"Ignite your senses at Candle, where flickering candlelight and a curated menu of globally-inspired dishes create an enchanting dining experience like no other."

Candle is more than just a restaurant; it's a sanctuary for food enthusiasts and connoisseurs alike. Step into a world where every detail is meticulously crafted to ensure a truly extraordinary dining experience. The moment you enter Candle, you'll be enveloped in an atmosphere of warmth and elegance. The soft glow of candlelight dances across the room, casting a mesmerizing ambiance that invites you to unwind and indulge. The stylish decor, with its blend of contemporary design elements and rustic accents, creates a harmonious space that is both sophisticated and welcoming.
